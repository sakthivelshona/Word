Data Collection

We meticulously acquired an extensive dataset to power our text extraction application. Our character dataset comprises 3500 examples of English characters and digits, ensuring a comprehensive coverage of symbols. Additionally, we gathered a vast pool of 115,321 handwritten word samples (IAM dataset), encompassing various writing styles and variations.

Image Preprocessing

Prior to model training, we subjected the collected handwritten images to a rigorous preprocessing pipeline. This encompassed noise reduction, contrast enhancement, and image normalization techniques, all aimed at improving image quality and ensuring consistency in data presentation.

Label Encoding

To equip our model with the ability to recognize and interpret handwritten content accurately, we systematically assigned unique labels to each image. These labels were aligned with a predefined character list, creating a direct correspondence between image data and their actual content.

Data Splitting

We divided our dataset thoughtfully, with 90% of the data allocated for model training and the remaining 10% reserved for testing. This balanced split allowed us to assess the model's performance comprehensively and gauge its ability to generalize to new data.

Data Training

Leveraging state-of-the-art machine learning or deep learning methods, we embarked on the training phase. Our model underwent extensive iterations, learning to recognize and extract textual information from handwritten samples. This training process aimed to optimize recognition accuracy and minimize errors.

Image Segmentation

To precisely isolate individual characters or words within the handwritten images, we implemented advanced image segmentation algorithms. This step was essential for ensuring that our model could accurately delineate and interpret each component of the text.

Prediction

With a fully trained model with accuracy of 94% at our disposal, we seamlessly integrated it into our web application, enabling real-time predictions. Users can now upload handwritten images, and our system will swiftly and accurately extract text, making our application an invaluable asset for various text recognition tasks.
